Services

What can I do for you?

Technology Due Diligence, Leckie-Kershaw

With over 20 years' experience working with startups at seed and scale-up stages on both sides of the fundraising process, Leckie-Kershaw provides VC and PE houses, technology partners and angel investors with a concise due diligence package, working on hundreds of successful investment rounds for investors including Gresham House, YFM, Octopus Investments, Quona Capital and Maven Capital Partners.
This includes review and analysis of:

  • product delivery capability, culture and processes
  • product, implementation and data architecture
  • software development practises
  • feature roadmap
  • team health, tech debt and risk mitigation

Strategy Consultancy

If you've got a short-to-medium term need for strategic planning on technology, product roadmap, team growth and platform scaling, Leckie-Kershaw can give your team direction and to put in place a plan for growth. Whether you've got an idea and seed capital or you're heading towards a Series A or B round, we can provide non-executive, consulting CTO services to make sure you're on the right track and prepared for investors' and clients' due diligence, and long-term product stability.

Product, Roadmap and UX Architecture

With a wealth of experience as Product Owner, user interaction analyst and systems architect, I can give you valuable input and guidance to shape your product, end-user journey and systems architecture. Services include:

  • UI / UX analysis and usability consulting - increase engagement and conversions, reduce friction
  • Customer journey planning - smooth out interaction points and maximise positive user outcomes
  • Cloud architecture and platform scaling - 20+ years' experience designing digital platforms and mobile apps, including Android, iOS, web apps and cloud services with Amazon AWS, Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud
  • Roadmap planning - optimise your product development pipeline, with competitor analysis and best-in-market tailoring of your feature set

Clients

Selected work I've done

Technology Due Diligence

Since 2019, I've been providing due diligence reporting services for global VC and PE houses. The scope of each project varies, but broadly, I delivery a report based on thorough discovery with the target company's product and technology delivery teams to assess their team health, risks and roadmap delivery capability post-investment.

Recent reporting projects include:

  • eConsult for Gresham House - healhcare technology specialist adding efficiency to triage and remote assessments
  • Adimo for Maven Capital Partners - adtech provider specialising in retail shopping ad optimisation
  • Perfect Ward for Octopus Investments - improving clinical facility assessments for the NHS and other providers

CTO and Head of Product, Corethree

After co-founding Corethree, I spent 8 years as Chief Technology officer alongside my two co-founders. I was responsible for engineering, product development and R&D, and had a hand in virtually every technical sales process and contract closed.

Identifying a market gap for a robust, UX-first rapid systems integration platform, I architected and built Core Engine, a bespoke microservices solution on AWS, MongoDB and Microsoft.NET. Core Engine has since driven the company's growth to the UK and Europe's leading mobile ticketing platform for transport providers.

  • Led scoping, technical sales, architecture and delivery for 50+ B2B and B2C projects
  • Sold in and delivered Transport for London's Santander Cycles mobile app and back-end platform
  • Developed and managed Corethree's market-leading M-Tickets transport ticketing platform, with millions of tickets sold since launch
  • Drove smart cities projects with partners including IBM, Keolis Global and the UK's Department for Transport

Consulting Architect, Octopus Investments

I was engaged by Octopus Labs, the group's IT and engineering Department, to conduct in-depth analysis of their estate of cloud computing assets. Over the course of 11 months, I put in place custom cost and resource reporting systems and worked with the Engineering team to begin the transition away from monolithic application hosting to containerised, Kubernetes-powered deployments.

I also worked with Octopus Ventures to provide due diligence analysis for technology startups in their investment pipeline.

  • Integrated Amazon AWS, Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure with Labs' CloudHealth cost reporting platform
  • Deployed a cloud asset lifecycle tracking solution to maintain detailed resource metadata for everything running in the cloud
  • Performed technology due diligence services, evaluating startup investment opportunities for Octopus Ventures

Founding CTO, Electric Playbox (formerly Immersive Games Lab)

Hired as employee #2 at this new retail entertainment business from the co-founder of Tough Mudder, I focused on taking a simple idea to proof-of-concept and fundraising.

Over 4 months, I conducted R&D in the immersive games and escape room industry, building prototypes and running user testing before finalising an entirely original software/hardware platform for room-scale, team video gaming.

  • Reported to CEO, leading roadmap, product and delivery
  • Took the company from startup to post-seed fundraising and retail launch
  • Helped to close a £2.6m seed round with Index Ventures, Sweet Capital and JamJar as lead investors
  • Architecture and software development for back-office and infrastructure
  • Worked with games developers to tie the room tech into the Unity games engine

Trustee and Non-executive Director, Sunday Assembly

I sat on the Board of Trustees as technology strategy consultant for Sunday Assembly, a global community charity founded in London in 2013. The Board oversees strategic planning, financial management, due diligence and oversight of the London-based charity body responsible for supporting the community and the events it runs for its members.

  • Provided digital communications strategy to the Board and formalised much of their community engagement processes
  • Consolidated their infrastructure, migrating their web presence to a modern platform
  • Worked as part of the team formalising community engagement into a continuous feedback process
