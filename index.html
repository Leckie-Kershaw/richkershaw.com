About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Rich Kershaw I’m a technology consultant and product architect in London, UK. I’m a maker, designer and builder of things.

Among other things, over the past 10 years, I've:

  • Designed the UX and built the infrastructure for Transport for London's Cycle Hire mobile bike hire app
  • Prototyped and built room-scale immersive group gaming experiences to the high street for Immersive Games Lab (now Electric Playhouse), helping them gain seed funding from Index Ventures and Sweet Capital
  • Run UX development, architecture and project delivery for over 50 individual projects as CTO at Corethree
  • Worked with IBM, Deloitte, the Department for Transport, Capita, Serco and Transport for London on transformative projects like Santander Cycles
  • Led cloud architecture for Octopus Investments' in-house Octopus Labs team

I'm currently available for short and long-term projects, including interim and non-executive CTO positions.
