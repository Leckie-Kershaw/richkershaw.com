About Me

Who Am I?

Hi. I'm Rich Kershaw. I’m a technology consultant and product architect in London, UK.
I’m a maker, designer and builder of things.

Among other things, over the past 15 years, I've:

  • Led tech due diligence for vendors, clients and investors on many dozens of projects
  • Designed the UX and built the infrastructure for Transport for London's Cycle Hire mobile bike hire app
  • Prototyped and built immersive group gaming experiences for Electric Playhouse (the Tough Mudder founder's next project), helping close seed funding from Index Ventures and Sweet Capital
  • Run UX development, architecture and project delivery for over 50 individual projects as CTO at Corethree
  • Worked with IBM, Deloitte, the Department for Transport, Capita, Serco and Transport for London on transformative projects like Santander Cycles
  • Led cloud architecture for Octopus Investments' in-house Octopus Labs team

These days, I consult with startups, SMEs and investors on technology and product strategy, scaling and due diligence.

I'm currently available for tech due diligence projects, interim and non-executive CTO positions and product strategy.

Services

What can I do for you?

Technology Due Diligence

With over 15 years' experience working with startups at seed and scale-up stages on both sides of the fundraising process, I provide VC houses, technology partners and angel investors with a concise due diligence package.
This includes review and analysis of:

  • product, implementation and data architecture
  • software development practises
  • feature roadmap
  • team health and risk mitigation
The tech DD process will take you through to Heads of Terms ahead of later stages like codebase and IP review.

Non-executive Directorship

Having served as both executive and non-executive director in the technology industry for over 15 years, I can bring my practical, real-world experience of building and scaling B2B and B2C products to your board. Regardless of the stage your company is at right now, my enormous breadth of knowledge at the intersection of commercial and technology strategy can add an extra dimension to your planning and management.

Interim CTO, Strategy Consultancy

If you've got a short-to-medium term need for strategic leadership on technology, product roadmap, team growth and platform scaling, I'm here to give your team direction and to put in place a plan for growth. Whether you've got an idea and seed capital or you're heading towards a Series A or B round, I can provide non-executive, interim or consulting CTO services to make sure you're on the right track and prepared for investors' and clients' due diligence, and long-term product stability.

Product, Roadmap and UX Architecture

With a wealth of experience as Product Owner, user interaction analysis and systems architecture, I can give you valuable input and guidance to shape your product, end-user journey and systems architecture. Services include:

  • UI / UX analysis and usability consulting - increase engagement and conversions, reduce friction
  • Customer journey planning - smooth out interaction points and maximise positive user outcomes
  • Cloud architecture and plaform scaling - 20+ years' experience designing digital platforms and mobile apps, including Android, iOS, web apps and cloud services with Amazon AWS, Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud
  • Roadmap planning - optimise your product development pipeline, with competitor analysis and best-in-market tailoring of your feature set
